This ArcGIS tool creates a shapefile that displays the Ecological Site Description determined by the Soil Survey Geographic Database. 

This may give a general sense of what a site's historic ecological community was like or what its potential for restoration is. It is important to note that this is only as useful as the available data, and that soil ecological site descriptions do not always reflect the plant community present at a location prior to land use changes. For more information visit: https://www.nrcs.usda.gov/getting-assistance/technical-assistance/ecological-sciences/ecological-site-descriptions


To Use:
1. Download the tool and add it to your ArcGIS project.
2. Download SSURGO data for your specified area of interest from Web Soil Survey: https://websoilsurvey.sc.egov.usda.gov/app/
3. Open the tool in ArcGIS and enter the required shapefiles and tables (from spatial and tabular folders, respectively)
4. Optionally, you may enter road or stream shapefiles to also be clipped to the area of interest
5. Run the tool
